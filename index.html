Math Dungeon

"The math was stolen and a brave explorer must dive into a misterious adventure to recover it". Math Dungeon was my first experience with videogames development. It all started when I joined a small team during a game jam and that team later became a startup named NapsyGames!

3D Art

My main role in the team was the 3D Artist. Indeed I made the 3D models, the textures and many of the animations seen in the game. Our goal was to develop a mobile game for children to learn maths more easily.

Growth

The fact that it was my first experience in this field, it taught me very much. Firstly, it improved my 3D animation and 3D modeling skills, e.g.: by paying attention at keeping a number of polygons lower than a certain limit, but it also taught me how to work in a team. Team work is not easy: many tasks to synchronize and deadlines to respect, so it gave me the first taste of that reality.

A hard lesson

Even though we were a group of young and talented guys, unfortunately the startup did not last very much and we had to split up. But this event did not stop my will to become a game developer and I made a treasure of the experience that this journey helped me to develop.
